PRODUCTS
Let's talk about what we sell.

We have 6 bike options for $399. They are all very light. They have a stand for a bottle. Their frame is made of durable metals.
Their wheels are made by our company. Unfortunately, they do not have disc pads, but we are planning to release a
new collection with disc brakes.

SERVICES
Let's talk about how we ship and package the product.

We do not send bikes to avoid problems with postal services. You can order a bike and we will prepare it for you. You can pick it up at our office, which is listed in your contacts.
We pack the bike frame with a special film to prevent it from getting scratched. You can pay online or on the spot. For an additional fee, we can replace certain parts
to customise the bike for you. Also, if you travel more in mountainous landscapes, we will replace your wheels with mountain wheels and
change the suspension mode to hard for free.

OUR TEAM
Let's talk about who we are.

The word "we" means a large team of 20+ people. We work every day except Sunday. Our team works from 9am to 8pm. You can contact our manager by email
or phone number. But our manager does not take orders, but only informs people about our products. We have more than 3 managers so you don't have to wait to get
an answer to your question. If you want to evaluate our work, you can send an email and we will attach your comment to our website.
